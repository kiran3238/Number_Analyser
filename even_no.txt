                
                
                An even number is an integer that  is
                
                exactly divisible by 2,meaning it has
                
                no remainder  when  divided  by  2.In
                
                other words, if you can divide number
                
                by 2 and get  a whole  number  result,
                
                then that number is even.

                Example:-

                2,4,6,8...
